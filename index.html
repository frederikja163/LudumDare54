Antlantis

Welcome to the world of antlantis. This game is about building an ant colony. On the bottom you can choose between digging and filling, you can also pick a type of chamber to set the type of any chamber you click on. Click on the egg to choose what ants to create. There are 4 types of ants viewed on the top. Queen: You always have one of these, if you lose her you lose the game. Farmer: Did you know ants in real life farm mushrooms? This is your source of food if you have enough farm chambers. Worker: It takes a lot of work to maintain the ant colony, each worker allows you to have more rooms dug out. Soldier: These are used to combat outside threats. Don't get too comfortable while idling in this idle city builder, or you might lose your ants to a random event.
